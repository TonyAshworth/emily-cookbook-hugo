**Bahn Mi Rice Bowls**

* Pork tenderloin
* ¼ C soy sauce
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 Tbs brown sugar

Mix all together in crock pot and cook on low for 6-8 hours. Shred pork and serve over rice, excellent with shredded carrots and/or purple cabbage as a topper!
