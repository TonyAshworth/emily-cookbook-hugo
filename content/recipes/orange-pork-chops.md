* 1 C orange juice
* 2 Tbs red currant jelly
* ½ C brown sugar
* 4-6 bs pork chops

In skillet, brown chops. Mix ingredients and add to pan. Simmer covered for 20 minutes or so, then 15 minutes more uncovered. Sauce should thicken. Yummy over white rice!
