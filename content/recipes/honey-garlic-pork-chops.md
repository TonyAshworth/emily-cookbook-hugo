* 4-6 bs pork chops
* ½ C ketchup
* ¼ C honey
* 2 tsp minced garlic
* ⅓ C soy sauce
* 2 Tbs cornstarch mixed in ¼ C water

Whisk all but cornstarch slurry in crock pot, then add meat and coat. Cook on low for 4-5 hours. Add cornstarch slurry and cook for one more hour.
