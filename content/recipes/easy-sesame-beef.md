In bowl: 

* ¼ C soy sauce
* 2 Tbs sesame seeds
* 5 Tbs sugar

In skillet: 

* 1-2 lbs sliced steak
* Dash olive oil
* Dash garlic powder

Brown beef in skillet, then pour sauce over and simmer just until it thickens. Yummy served over rice!
